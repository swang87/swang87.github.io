Introduction

Born in Nanjing, China, I moved to the United States at a young age. I have spent a great deal of my life in Toronto, Canada (eh); Boca Raton, FL; Berkeley, CA; and New Haven, CT.

Fresh out of my undergraduate studies at Berkeley, I spent some time working as an actuary in a large consulting firm. After a while, I thirsted for something more cutting edge, and joined an exciting new startup then called WeatherBill (now called The Climate Corporation). There, I did a lot of work on pricing weather derivatives and analyzing potential clients' weather risks.

I greatly enjoyed data analysis and working with large amounts of data, but in order to work more effectively, I needed to go back to school. Now I am almost ready to get back to work!
