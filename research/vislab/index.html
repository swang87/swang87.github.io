In a paper titled ``Data Visualization on Day One: Bringing Big Ideas into Intro Stats Early and Often'' (coauthored with Cynthia Rush and Nicholas Horton; accepted to Technology Innovations in Statistics Education), we describe a lab activity that aims to promote multivariate thinking with R computing on the first day of class. This page contains examples of student work from this lab activity.
